📋 Features Guide

🏠
Dashboard — Overview stats and recent admissions at a glance.
👥
View Students — Search, filter, view, edit and delete student records.
➕
Add Student — Multi-section form to register a new student.
📊
Reports — Bar & donut charts plus detailed department/fee/attendance tables.

Student Profile — Full profile with results, fees, attendance summary.
